Questions

Got questions? Totally got answers.

Is this a replacement for the Bible?

No — it is a modern paraphrase and creative commentary. We always encourage reading a traditional translation alongside this one. Think of it like a conversation about Scripture, not a substitute for it.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes — monthly and annual subscriptions can be canceled at any time from your account page. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.

What does the 15-day guarantee cover?

Annual subscriptions come with a full 15-day money back guarantee. If you are not satisfied for any reason within 15 days we will refund you completely.

Is my payment secure?

Yes — all payments are processed securely through Stripe. We never store your card information on our servers.

What translation is this based on?

Every verse is rooted in the original King James Version text — which is in the public domain — and re-imagined in modern, relatable language with a sincere heart.

Can I use this in my church or ministry?

Please reach out before using our content in public settings. We are happy to discuss licensing and permissions for ministry use.

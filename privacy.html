Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026
The Bible, Like Totally is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit https://thebibleliketotally.com and any associated platforms including our YouTube channel and email communications. By using our Website you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1 Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you create an account, subscribe to our email list, purchase a subscription, contact us, or submit feedback. This may include:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe. We do not store full payment card numbers.
  • Membership and subscription status
  • Any other information you choose to provide

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Website we may automatically collect technical information including IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time spent on each page, referring URL, date and time of your visit, and device type. This information is collected through cookies and tracking technologies described in Section 5.

1.3 Information From Third Parties

We may receive information from Stripe when you complete a payment, Google Analytics when you interact with our Website, and Kit (our email marketing platform) when you interact with our emails.

2 How We Use Your Information

To Provide and Manage the Service

  • Create and manage your account through ProfilePress
  • Process subscription payments through Stripe
  • Deliver access to paid and free content
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Send transactional emails such as receipts and account notifications

To Communicate With You

  • Send our email newsletter if you have subscribed
  • Notify you of updates to our content, Terms of Use, or Privacy Policy
  • Respond to feedback and contact form submissions

To Improve the Website

  • Analyze how visitors use the Website through Google Analytics
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Improve content, design, and user experience

🔒 We do not sell your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

3 How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information only with the following trusted service providers:

💳 Stripe — Payment Processing

Processes all payment transactions. We do not store your full payment card information.

View Stripe's Privacy Policy →

👤 ProfilePress — Membership & User Profiles

Manages user registration, login, membership levels, and profile data stored in our secure WordPress database.

📧 Kit (formerly ConvertKit) — Email Marketing

Manages our email subscriber list. When you subscribe, your name and email are stored securely with Kit.

View Kit's Privacy Policy →

📊 Google Analytics — Website Analytics

Helps us understand Website traffic using anonymous usage data via cookies.

View Google's Privacy Policy →

We may also disclose your information if required by law, to protect our rights, or in the event of a business transfer. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4 Data Retention

  • Account information is retained for the duration of your account and a reasonable period afterward
  • Payment records are retained as required by applicable tax and financial regulations
  • Email subscriber information is retained until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention settings

We will delete or anonymize your personal information upon request as described in Section 7.

5 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. We use the following types:

  • Essential Cookies — required for the Website to function properly including your login session and payment processing. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies — Google Analytics uses these to collect anonymous usage data. You may opt out.
  • Payment Cookies — Stripe may place cookies necessary to process payments securely.

You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6 Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Users between the ages of 13 and 17 must have parental consent to create an account.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent we will take steps to delete that information promptly. Please contact us immediately if you believe this has occurred.

7 Your Rights & Choices

We honor the following rights for all users regardless of location:

Access & Portability

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Correction

Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information subject to legal obligations.

Opt Out of Email

Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time via any email we send.

🔒 We do not sell your personal information. If this policy ever changes we will notify you and provide an opt-out mechanism before any sale occurs.

8 California Residents — CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you additional rights:

  • Right to Know — what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell about you
  • Right to Delete — request deletion of your personal information subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to Opt Out — opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

To exercise your CCPA rights please contact us through our Contact page. We will respond to verified requests within 45 days.

9 European Users — GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland the GDPR provides you with additional rights.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract — processing necessary to fulfill our subscription agreement with you
  • Legitimate Interests — improving the Website and preventing fraud
  • Consent — subscribing to our email newsletter
  • Legal Obligation — complying with applicable law

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your personal information. You also have the right to data portability and to withdraw consent at any time. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Our Website is operated in the United States. By using the Website you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States.

10 Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information including:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe — we never store full payment card numbers
  • Membership and account data secured through ProfilePress
  • Access controls limiting who can access personal information

However no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. If you believe your information has been compromised please contact us immediately.

11 Third-Party Websites & Platforms

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites and platforms including our YouTube channel, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. This Privacy Policy applies only to our Website at https://thebibleliketotally.com. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party platforms and encourage you to review their individual privacy policies.

12 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make changes we will update the Last Updated date at the top of this page. For significant changes we will provide more prominent notice such as an email notification to subscribers.

Your continued use of the Website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

13 Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices please contact us. We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

Questions about your privacy or data? We are here to help.

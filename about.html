About This Translation

A totally chill take on timeless truth.

Why The Bible, Like Totally exists — and why it might be exactly what you have been looking for.

Welcome to The Bible, Like Totally

Welcome to The Bible, Like Totally — a creative and modern expression of God's Word told through a relatable, chill vibe. The goal isn't to change Scripture, but to help people hear it in a voice that feels familiar, modern, and surprisingly relatable.

The style is playful, yes — but the heart behind it is completely sincere. Every verse is rooted in the original King James Version text and re-imagined with lighthearted language, modern expressions, and a gentle wink of fun, while always honoring the core meaning of Scripture.

"A totally chill take on timeless truth."

The tagline says it all. Chill on the outside. Sincere all the way through.

Why This Translation Exists

There are a lot of translations of The Bible — and each one helps somebody "hear" God in a certain way. This version was created for people who might feel intimidated by older language, or just want a refreshing, approachable way to read God's Word without losing the beauty and power behind it.

Maybe you're new to reading The Bible. Maybe you grew up with church but drifted away. Maybe you're just curious about what all the fuss is about. Whoever you are — this is for you. And the door is wide open.

How It's Made

This project is a verse-by-verse journey through Scripture, using fun and relatable slang, with gentle humor woven throughout — BUT always built on the foundation of the original Bible text.

You'll notice:

  • Everyday conversational tone that sounds like a real person talking
  • Natural "likes" woven in throughout — because that's how we actually talk
  • Playful color and voice that makes each chapter feel alive
  • Creative vibe on the outside, respectful heart on the inside

Each chapter is carefully transformed to keep the original meaning while making the language feel like something we would actually say in real life today.

A Note of Reverence

This is not a replacement for The Bible.
It's a modern paraphrase.

Think of it like a commentary in the form of a conversation — a way to experience Scripture with fresh ears, not a substitute for Scripture itself. You are encouraged to read traditional Scripture translations alongside this one.

Our Hope For You

More than anything, our prayer is that reading Scripture here makes you smile, think, and feel a little closer to God. May these playful words make His truth feel alive, welcoming, and beautifully timeless — whether you're here for curiosity, encouragement, study, or just a fresh spiritual vibe.

Thanks for being here. Grab a verse. Open your heart. And let's read The Bible, Like Totally — in a voice that feels totally righteous for you today.
